Chapter One
🎨 The Hidden Colours
✅ Read! One rainy evening, Aslan was sitting by his window, watching the last drops of rain fall from the sky. The clouds were clearing. Then something strange happened. High above the rooftops, colours began to appear. Red. Orange. Yellow. Green. Blue. Violet. It was a rainbow — but not like any rainbow Aslan had ever seen. It was glowing and shimmering. It looked like a bridge. Aslan ran to call his mother, but she could not see it. Only Aslan could see the magical bridge in the sky. It was his — and his alone.

Chapter Two
🌈 Crossing the Rainbow Bridge
✅ Read! The next morning, Aslan woke up early. The rainbow bridge was still there, shining in the morning light. Aslan took a deep breath and reached out with his hand. The bridge felt warm and solid, like sunlight made into a floor. He stepped onto it. The colours sparkled under his feet. Every step he took made a soft musical sound, like tiny bells. Aslan walked higher and higher into the sky. Below him, the village looked as small as a toy. Above him, the sky opened up like a door — and beyond it was a world of pure colour.

shining solid sunlight sparkled musical bells village
03
Chapter Three
🌟 The Land of Colours
✅ Read! On the other side of the bridge, everything was different. The trees were blue. The flowers were golden. The rivers ran with liquid emerald. The clouds above were pink and soft as cotton. Aslan gasped. He had never seen such beautiful colours. Small creatures made of pure light danced around him. They had no words, only colours. When they were happy, they turned gold. When they were excited, they flashed every colour at once. Aslan laughed with joy. "This is the most magical place I have ever seen!" he shouted, and his voice echoed across the shining world.

emerald cotton gasped creatures danced flashed echoed
04
Chapter Four
⛈️ The Colour Storm
✅ Read! Suddenly, the sky began to change. Dark clouds rolled in. The little light creatures turned grey with fear. A colour storm was coming! Bolts of coloured lightning lit up the sky — red, blue, purple. The colours of the land began to fade. Aslan felt afraid. But then he remembered something his teacher always said: "Imagination is the most powerful thing in the world. Use it." Aslan closed his eyes. He thought of the most beautiful colours he could imagine. He thought of sunsets and fireflies. Slowly, warm light began to spread from his hands.

storm lightning fade imagination sunsets fireflies powerful
05
Chapter Five
💜 The Bridge Disappears
✅ Read! The storm faded away. The little creatures turned gold again and danced around Aslan with joy. But then, the rainbow bridge began to fade. It was disappearing! One of the light creatures touched Aslan's hand gently. It flashed a deep warm orange — the colour of courage. Aslan understood. The bridge was not gone. It had moved inside him. He walked back home, and when he picked up his crayons and began to draw, the colours poured out like magic. The rainbow bridge was not in the sky. It was in his heart, his hands, and his imagination — and it would always be there for him.
