My Family and Me
📖 Mendeskripsikan anggota keluarga & peran mereka 🔤 Present Simple 📚 Family Vocabulary
Bacaan

Hello! My name is Aslan. I am a student. I live in Bandung with my family. I really love my city and my home.

My father's name is Sigit. He is a very creative and talented person. He is a businessman, a musician, an IT expert, and also a content creator. He is very busy, but he always has time for our family. I think my father is amazing!

My mother's name is Fety. She is a primary school teacher at SD Cemara. She is very hardworking and caring. She loves teaching children every day. She always makes sure our family is healthy and happy.

I have one little sister. Her name is Alana. She is almost three years old — her birthday is in October! She is very cute and funny. She loves to play and run around the house. I am a proud big brother!

We also have a grandfather and a grandmother who live with us. My grandfather's name is Kakek Cecep. He is wise and funny. My grandmother's name is Enin Ii. She always cooks delicious food for our family. Her cooking is the best!

I love my family very much. We always spend time together on weekends. We are a happy family.

Grammar Fokus
✦ Present Simple — Mendeskripsikan Orang & Keluarga
My father IS a content creator.
→ He / She + is + pekerjaan/profesi
He IS kind and patient.
→ He / She + is + kata sifat
My brother LIKES football.
→ He / She + likes + hobi
She COOKS delicious food.
→ He / She + kata kerja + -s
We ARE happy together.
→ We / They / You + are + kata sifat
💡 Ingat: Gunakan IS untuk he / she / it. Gunakan ARE untuk we / they / you. Tambahkan -s atau -es pada kata kerja setelah he / she / it — misalnya: likes, cooks, lives, teaches.
Percakapan
Teman — Rafi
Hey Aslan! How many people are in your family?
Aslan
There are six people in my family — my father Sigit, my mother Fety, my little sister Alana, my grandfather Kakek Cecep, and my grandmother Enin Ii. We all live together in Bandung!
Teman — Rafi
Wow, that's a big family! What does your father do?
Aslan
My father Sigit is very talented! He is a businessman, a musician, an IT expert, and also a content creator. He does many things and he is really amazing!
Teman — Rafi
Wow! What about your mother?
Aslan
My mother Fety is a primary school teacher at SD Cemara. She loves teaching children. She is very hardworking and caring.
Teman — Rafi
Do you have a little sister?
Aslan
Yes! Her name is Alana. She is almost three years old — her birthday is in October! She is so cute and funny. I love being her big brother!
