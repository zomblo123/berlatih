How's the Weather Today?
📖 Kosakata cuaca & perkiraan cuaca sederhana 🔤 It is / It's + adjective 📚 Weather Vocabulary
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan and I live in Bandung. Bandung is famous for its cool and fresh weather. People always say, "Bandung is the city of flowers." I think it is also the city of perfect weather!

In the morning, the weather in Bandung is usually cool and foggy. It feels very fresh when I wake up at six o'clock. Sometimes I can see a thin layer of mist near the mountains. Kakek Cecep loves the cool morning air — he always does his gardening in the morning!

During the dry season, the days are sunny and warm. It is a great time to play football at the park. Fathan and I love playing outside when it is sunny. But in Bandung, even on a sunny day, it is never too hot. It is always comfortable!

During the rainy season, it often rains in the afternoon. The rain in Bandung can be very heavy sometimes. When it rains, I come home from school and stay inside. I enjoy reading books or playing Minecraft with Fathan while it rains outside. Enin Ii always makes a warm drink for us!

My favourite weather is when it is cloudy but not raining. It is cool and comfortable — perfect for a walk. I also love the weather just after it rains. Everything smells so fresh and the sky looks beautiful.

What about you? What is your favourite weather? In Bandung, we are very lucky — the weather is almost always pleasant. That is one reason why I love my city so much!

Grammar Fokus
✦ It is / It's + Adjective — Mendeskripsikan Cuaca
It is sunny today.
→ It is / It's + adjective cuaca
It's raining outside.
→ It's + kata kerja -ing (sedang terjadi)
It's going to rain later.
→ It's going to + kata kerja (prediksi)
How's the weather today?
→ How's = How is (pertanyaan cuaca)
What's the weather like?
→ What's the weather like? (alternatif pertanyaan)
💡 Selalu gunakan "It" saat mendeskripsikan cuaca — bukan "The weather is" saja (meski benar, "It's" lebih natural dan umum digunakan).

☀️ It's sunny.  |  🌧️ It's raining.  |  ☁️ It's cloudy.  |  💨 It's windy.

Tanya: "How's the weather?"  →  Jawab: "It's [adjective]."
Percakapan
Fathan
Hey Aslan! How's the weather in Bandung today?
Aslan
It's cloudy and cool this morning — typical Bandung! There was some fog near the mountains when I woke up. It's so beautiful and fresh. Kakek Cecep is already in the garden!
Fathan
Do you think it's going to rain this afternoon? Should I bring an umbrella?
Aslan
Probably yes! In Bandung, it usually rains in the afternoon during the rainy season. Better bring your umbrella. But the morning looks nice — it might be sunny later!
Fathan
What's your favourite kind of weather, Aslan?
Aslan
I love it when it's cloudy but not raining — cool and comfortable. Perfect for a walk or playing football. But I also love after-rain weather! Everything smells so fresh. What about you?
Fathan
I like sunny weather! Then we can play football at the park. If it rains, we just play Minecraft inside!
Aslan
Haha! That's true. Rain or shine — we always have fun! And Enin Ii's warm drinks make rainy days even better. 😄

Tips Belajar Hari Ini
🌤️
Jadikan Cuaca sebagai Latihan Harian!
Mulai sekarang, setiap pagi sebelum berangkat sekolah, lihat ke luar jendela dan ucapkan cuaca hari itu dalam bahasa Inggris:

☀️ "It's sunny today! I don't need an umbrella."
🌧️ "It's raining. I should bring my umbrella."
☁️ "It's cloudy and cool — typical Bandung morning!"

Kamu juga bisa bilang ke Kakek Cecep atau Ibu Fety setiap pagi: "The weather today is..." Latihan kecil setiap hari = kemajuan besar! 🌱
