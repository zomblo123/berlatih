A Letter to My Pen Pal
📖 Menulis surat pribadi dengan salam & penutup 🔤 Letter Format 📚 Simple Self-Introduction
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to tell you about something exciting — I have a pen pal! A pen pal is a friend in another city or country that you write letters to. My pen pal's name is Daniel and he lives in Yogyakarta.

We became pen pals through a school programme. My teacher at school helped me find a pen pal. Writing letters in English is a great way to practise! I was a little nervous at first, but my father Sigit encouraged me. He said, "Just write from your heart, Aslan!"

In my first letter, I introduced myself. I wrote about my family — my father Sigit who is a musician and content creator, my mother Fety who is a teacher at SD Cemara, my little sister Alana who is almost three, and my grandparents Kakek Cecep and Enin Ii. I also wrote about my best friend Fathan, my hobbies, and my city Bandung.

Daniel replied quickly! He told me about his family in Yogyakarta, his school, and his hobbies. He loves drawing traditional batik patterns and playing badminton. He also told me that Yogyakarta is famous for its culture and the beautiful Prambanan temple.

Writing letters has taught me many things. I learn new words, practise my grammar, and also learn about life in other places. I feel very excited every time a letter from Daniel arrives!

Do you want to have a pen pal too? I think it is a wonderful idea. You make a new friend and practise your English at the same time. That is the best kind of learning!

Surat Aslan kepada Daniel
🇮🇩 BANDUNG
✉️ From: Aslan · Bandung, West Java
Sunday, 17th May 2026

Dear Daniel,

Hello! My name is Aslan. I am so happy to have you as my pen pal! I hope this letter finds you well.

Let me tell you a little about myself. I am a student and I live in Bandung, West Java. Bandung is a beautiful city with cool and fresh weather. I love living here!

I live with my family. My father's name is Sigit. He is a very creative man — he is a businessman, a musician, an IT expert, and a content creator. My mother's name is Fety. She is a primary school teacher at SD Cemara. They are both very hardworking and kind.

I also have a little sister named Alana. She is almost three years old and her birthday is in October. She is the cutest and funniest person in our house! My grandfather Kakek Cecep loves gardening, and my grandmother Enin Ii is the best cook in Bandung — I am not joking!

My best friend's name is Fathan. He loves playing football and Minecraft. We walk home from school together every day and always stop at the warung near my house to buy snacks.

My hobbies are reading books, cycling, and listening to music. My father sometimes teaches me to play guitar, which is really fun!

I am very excited to learn about you and Yogyakarta. Please write back soon!

Your new pen pal,
Aslan 🌟
✉️ Personal letter 📍 Bandung → Yogyakarta 🗓️ May 2026 🇮🇩 Indonesia

Grammar Fokus
✦ Self-Introduction — Memperkenalkan Diri dalam Surat
My name is Aslan.
→ Memperkenalkan nama: My name is + nama
I live in Bandung.
→ Menyebut tempat tinggal: I live in + kota
I am a student.
→ Menyebut identitas: I am + profesi/status
My father's name is Sigit.
→ Memperkenalkan orang lain: [nama]'s name is + nama
I hope this letter finds you well.
→ Kalimat pembuka surat yang sopan & natural
Please write back soon!
→ Kalimat penutup yang ramah — minta balasan
💡 Tips menulis surat: Gunakan Present Simple untuk fakta tentang dirimu. Gunakan kalimat pendek dan jelas. Selalu mulai dengan Dear [Nama], dan akhiri dengan [Frasa penutup], [Nama kamu]. Jangan lupa tulis tanggal di bagian atas!
Percakapan
Fathan
Hey Aslan! I heard you have a pen pal now. That's so cool! Who is he?
Aslan
Yes! His name is Daniel and he lives in Yogyakarta. We write letters to each other in English. It's really fun and great practice too!
Fathan
What do you write about in your letters?
Aslan
I wrote about myself — my family, Bandung, my hobbies like reading and cycling, and even about you! I told him my best friend loves football and Minecraft. He thought that was funny!
Fathan
Haha! What did Daniel write back about?
Aslan
He loves drawing traditional batik patterns and playing badminton! He told me about Yogyakarta — the culture, the food, and the Prambanan temple. It sounds amazing. I really want to visit someday!
Fathan
How do you start a letter in English? I want to try writing one too!
Aslan
Easy! Start with the date, then write "Dear [name]," then introduce yourself. End with "Your friend," and your name. The most important thing — just be yourself and write what you feel! My father Sigit always says: "Write from your heart." 😊
