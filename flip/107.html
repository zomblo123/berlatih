My Best Friend
📖 Mendeskripsikan penampilan & kepribadian seseorang 🔤 Adjectives 📚 Present Simple · Describing People
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to tell you about my best friend. His name is Fathan. We have been friends since the first grade. He is my closest friend at school in Bandung.

Fathan is a tall boy. He has short, black hair and dark eyes. He always wears a big smile on his face. He has two favourite hobbies — playing football with friends and playing Minecraft. He is very good at both!

Fathan has a wonderful personality. He is very funny and always makes everyone laugh. He is also very kind and helpful. When I don't understand something at school, he always explains it patiently. He is a very smart student too.

Fathan and I always walk home from school together. We pass the warung next to my house and buy snacks. We talk about everything — school, football tactics, Minecraft strategies, and even my father Sigit's YouTube videos! Fathan thinks my father is very cool because he is a musician and content creator.

Fathan also loves my family. He thinks Enin Ii's cooking is the best in Bandung. He always asks, "Is Enin Ii cooking today?" every time he comes to my house! And little Alana loves Fathan too — she always runs to him and asks him to play with her.

I am very lucky to have a friend like Fathan. A good friend is someone who is honest, supportive, and always there for you. Fathan is all of those things. I hope we will always be best friends forever!

Grammar Fokus
✦ Adjectives — Mendeskripsikan Orang dengan Present Simple
He is tall and athletic.
→ He / She + is + adjective (sifat)
He has short black hair.
→ He / She + has + adjective + noun (benda)
He always makes me laugh.
→ always / usually / never + kata kerja
He looks friendly.
→ looks / seems / appears + adjective
He is the funniest person I know.
→ superlative: the + adjective + -est
💡 Ingat: Adjective di bahasa Inggris selalu diletakkan SEBELUM kata benda — tidak seperti bahasa Indonesia!
✅ "short black hair"  |  ✅ "a funny boy"  |  ❌ "hair black short"

Yes, Miss! My best friend's name is Fathan. We have been friends since the first grade. He is a tall boy with short black hair and dark eyes. He loves playing football and Minecraft — he is amazing at both!

Tips Belajar Hari Ini
👫
Deskripsikan Orang-orang di Sekitarmu dalam Bahasa Inggris!
Pilih 3 orang yang kamu kenal baik — bisa teman, anggota keluarga, atau guru — lalu tulis deskripsi singkat dalam bahasa Inggris. Gunakan pola:

"[Nama] is [adjective personality]. He/She has [adjective] [noun — rambut/mata]. He/She always [kebiasaan]."

Contoh: "Fathan is funny and kind. He has short black hair. He always makes me laugh."

💡 Tantangan bonus: deskripsikan Ayah Sigit, Ibu Fety, Kakek Cecep, dan Enin Ii — masing-masing 2 kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris! 🌟
