Chapter One
🌙 Strange Noises Next Door
✅ Read! Aslan had just moved into a new house with his family on Maple Street. He liked his new bedroom, but there was one thing that worried him — the house next door. It was big and dark, with tall trees in front of it. Every night, Aslan heard strange noises coming from that house. Sometimes it sounded like a very low, deep voice. Sometimes it sounded like heavy footsteps on the floor. One evening, Aslan peeked through his bedroom window and saw a huge shadow moving inside the house next door. It was much bigger than any person he had ever seen. Aslan pulled his blanket up to his nose. "Maybe I should not look," he whispered to himself. But he could not help it. He was very, very curious.

Chapter Two
👾 Meeting the Big Monster
✅ Read! The next Saturday morning, Aslan's mother asked him to return a bowl of soup to their neighbor. "But Mom," said Aslan, "what if something scary lives there?" His mother just smiled. "Go on, Aslan. Be brave." Aslan walked slowly to the door next door and knocked three small times. The door opened — and Aslan looked up, and up, and up. Standing in the doorway was the biggest creature he had ever seen. It was tall and round and covered in soft purple fur. It had two large green eyes, small round ears on top of its head, and a mouth full of wide, friendly teeth. "HELLO!" said the monster in a very loud, deep voice. Aslan jumped back two steps. "H-hello," said Aslan, holding out the bowl with shaking hands.

Chapter Three
💜 The Kind Giant
✅ Read! The monster looked at the soup bowl and then looked at Aslan. Its two big green eyes went soft and warm. "Oh! Is this for me?" it said, in a voice that was loud but very gentle. "Thank you so very much! Nobody has ever brought me soup before." The monster carefully took the bowl with two huge purple fingers. Then it looked down at Aslan with a big smile and said, "My name is Grumbo. I am very happy to meet you! I know I look a little big and unusual, but I promise I am very friendly. Would you like to come inside for some tea and cookies?" Aslan blinked. The monster — Grumbo — did not look scary anymore. It looked... kind. And a little bit lonely. Aslan took a deep breath. "Yes," he said. "My name is Aslan. I would love some cookies."

Chapter Four
🙈 Playing Hide and Seek
✅ Read! Inside, Grumbo's house was warm and cozy. The chairs were big enough for a giant, and every shelf was full of books and little toy trains. Grumbo made a pot of tea and put out a plate of the biggest, softest cookies Aslan had ever seen. They talked for a long time. Aslan learned that Grumbo loved reading, gardening, and watching funny cartoons. After tea, Aslan had a great idea. "Grumbo, do you want to play hide and seek in the garden?" Grumbo's eyes went wide with excitement. "I have never played that before!" The game was very funny. When it was Grumbo's turn to hide, the big purple monster tried to squeeze behind a small tree. Of course, Aslan found Grumbo in about two seconds. They both laughed so hard that the neighbors across the street turned on their lights to see what the noise was.

Chapter Five
🌟 A New Best Neighbor
✅ Read! From that day on, Aslan and Grumbo became the best of neighbors. Every afternoon, Aslan would wave at Grumbo's window on his way home from school. Grumbo would wave back with one enormous purple hand. On weekends, they read books together in the garden. Grumbo helped Aslan reach the highest branches of the apple tree, and Aslan helped Grumbo learn new words from his English books. One day, Aslan's friend Rafi came to visit. He stopped at the gate when he saw Grumbo and looked very frightened. "Aslan, what is that?" he whispered. Aslan just smiled. "That is Grumbo, my neighbor and my friend. I know it seems surprising, but just say hello — you will see." Rafi took a deep breath, walked up, and said hello. Grumbo gave a giant warm smile and offered Rafi a cookie. That evening, Aslan thought about what he had learned. You should never judge someone by how they look. The biggest heart can live inside the most unusual body.
