My Favourite Book
📖 Mendeskripsikan cerita buku dengan kalimat sendiri 🔤 Story Plot · Title, Character, Setting 📚 Past Simple Narration
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan and today I want to tell you about my favourite book. As you know, I love reading. Every night before I sleep, I read at least a few pages. My mother Ibu Fety used to read me bedtime stories when I was small, and I think that is why I fell in love with books!

My favourite book is called "The Young Explorer of Borneo". It is an adventure story about a young boy named Rivan who lives in a village in Kalimantan. Rivan is brave, curious, and kind — a little like me, I hope! He is about the same age as me, which made the story feel very real.

The story begins when Rivan discovers a mysterious old map hidden inside a hollow tree near his village. The map showed a path through the deep rainforest to a hidden waterfall that nobody had visited in one hundred years. Rivan decided to find it.

The plot was full of exciting challenges. Rivan crossed a river on a raft he built himself. He climbed a steep hill in heavy rain. He met a wise old woman from a Dayak tribe who helped him find the right path. My favourite scene was when Rivan finally saw the waterfall — it was described as so beautiful that he just stood there and cried.

The ending was very touching. Rivan brought back a rare flower from near the waterfall — a flower that had healing properties. He gave it to the village healer to help sick people in his village. He didn't keep anything for himself. He just wanted to help.

I think this book is incredible because it shows that bravery is not about being fearless — it is about doing something important even when you are afraid. I read this book in one weekend and immediately wanted to read it again. I highly recommend it to everyone!

Struktur Cerita — Story Plot
1
📌 Title & Setting — Judul & Latar
Judul buku dan di mana / kapan cerita terjadi. "The Young Explorer of Borneo" berlatar di sebuah desa dan hutan hujan tropis di Kalimantan, Indonesia.
The story is set in a village in Kalimantan, Indonesia.
2
👤 Character — Tokoh
Siapa tokoh utamanya dan bagaimana sifatnya. Rivan adalah anak laki-laki yang berani, penasaran, dan baik hati — seusia Aslan.
The main character is Rivan. He is brave, curious, and kind.
3
🌀 Problem / Plot — Masalah / Alur
Konflik atau petualangan yang mendorong cerita. Rivan menemukan peta misterius dan memutuskan untuk mencari air terjun tersembunyi yang belum pernah dikunjungi selama 100 tahun.
Rivan found a mysterious map and decided to find a hidden waterfall.
4
⚡ Climax — Puncak Cerita
Momen paling menegangkan atau emosional. Saat Rivan akhirnya melihat air terjun yang indah setelah melewati semua tantangan — ia menangis terharu.
When Rivan finally saw the waterfall, he just stood there and cried.
5
✨ Ending — Akhir Cerita
Bagaimana cerita berakhir dan apa pesannya. Rivan membawa bunga langka untuk membantu orang sakit di desanya — bukan untuk kepentingan dirinya sendiri.
Rivan gave the rare flower to help sick people. He kept nothing for himself.

Grammar Fokus
✦ Past Simple Narration — Menceritakan Buku dengan Kalimat Lampau
The story begins when Rivan found a map.
→ Present untuk sinopsis + Past untuk kejadian dalam cerita
Rivan crossed a river on a raft.
→ Past simple untuk aksi tokoh dalam cerita
The story is set in Kalimantan.
→ Present untuk fakta tentang buku (judul, latar, tokoh)
My favourite part was when he saw the waterfall.
→ was when + past simple (momen favorit dalam cerita)
I think this book teaches us that...
→ Present untuk pendapat dan pesan buku
I highly recommend this book!
→ highly recommend = sangat merekomendasikan (frasa kuat)
💡 Tips mereview buku:
① Title + Genre: "This book is called... It is an adventure story."
② Character: "The main character is... He/She is..."
③ Setting: "The story is set in..."
④ Plot: "The story is about... [tokoh] [aksi] because [alasan]."
⑤ Opinion: "I think... / My favourite part was... / I recommend this because..."

Hi Daniel! My favourite book is called "The Young Explorer of Borneo." It's an adventure story set in Kalimantan. The main character is a boy named Rivan who is brave, curious, and kind — a bit like me, I hope! He found a mysterious old map and went on an incredible journey through the rainforest.
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Sounds amazing! What happened in the story?
Aslan
Rivan crossed rivers, climbed hills in heavy rain, and met a wise old woman from a Dayak tribe. He was searching for a hidden waterfall that nobody had visited in 100 years! My favourite part was when he finally found it — the book described it as so beautiful that Rivan just stood there and cried. I almost cried too!
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Wow! How did it end? Did he keep the treasure?
Aslan
No — and that's what I love most about the ending! He found a rare healing flower near the waterfall and brought it back to help sick people in his village. He kept nothing for himself. I think the book teaches us that true bravery is about helping others, not about personal glory. I highly recommend it, Daniel!
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
That sounds so meaningful! I will definitely look for that book. What is your favourite part of reading in general?
Aslan
I love how reading takes you to places you've never been! I've been to a rainforest in Kalimantan, a market in Cairo, and the moon — all without leaving my bed in Bandung! Ibu Fety always says: "A reader lives a thousand lives." I think that's so true. And reading in English gives me a thousand more! 📚
