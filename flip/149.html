Chapter One
🪶 The Glowing Feather
✅ Read! Early one morning, Aslan walked through the forest near his village. The air was cool and the birds were singing. Suddenly, something bright on the ground caught his eye. It was a feather — long, curved, and glowing with a deep orange and gold light. Aslan knelt down and gently picked it up. The feather was warm in his hand, like holding a tiny flame. Where it touched a wilted flower near his foot, the flower immediately bloomed again. Aslan gasped. This was no ordinary feather. He looked at the sky and wondered which creature could have dropped something so magical.

Chapter Two
🔥 The Burning Bird
✅ Read! An old woman who lived at the edge of the forest saw the feather in Aslan's hand. She gasped and covered her mouth. "That is a Phoenix feather! The Phoenix is the most sacred bird in the world. It is made of pure fire and lives for one thousand years." She explained that the Phoenix had been spotted flying weakly near the great volcano to the east. Without its feather, the Phoenix was losing its power and its warmth. Aslan held the feather tightly. He did not hesitate. I will return it, he said. The old woman smiled and gave him a small leather pouch to carry it safely. The journey to the volcano would take three days.

03
Chapter Three
🌋 The Journey to the Volcano
✅ Read! Aslan walked for many hours. He crossed a wide river on stepping stones. He climbed over rocky hills. At night, the feather inside the pouch glowed softly and kept him warm. On the second day, Aslan found a small deer with an injured leg. He gently pressed the feather against the deer's leg. A warm golden light spread over the wound. The deer stood up and leaped away into the trees, completely healed. Aslan smiled. The feather was not just beautiful — it was a gift of healing for the world. By the third day, he could see the volcano rising above the clouds.

04
Chapter Four
🪺 The Phoenix's Nest
✅ Read! Aslan climbed the steep side of the volcano. The rock was hot under his hands. At the top, on a wide ledge of black stone, he found the nest. It was built from gold branches and glowing embers. And there, curled inside it, was the Phoenix. Its feathers were dull and dim. Its eyes were half closed. It looked tired and weak. Aslan slowly opened the leather pouch. "I found your feather," he said in a soft voice. "I have come to return it." The Phoenix lifted its golden head and looked at Aslan with ancient, knowing eyes. A single tear of liquid fire rolled down its cheek.

05
Chapter Five
🌅 A Flame of Hope
✅ Read! Aslan carefully placed the feather back against the Phoenix's wing. The moment it touched, a burst of brilliant light exploded from the bird. Aslan shielded his eyes. When he looked again, the Phoenix stood tall, its feathers blazing with gold and crimson. It spread its magnificent wings wide. Then the Phoenix bowed its head slowly toward Aslan — a deep, graceful bow. It plucked one small feather from its own wing and placed it gently in Aslan's hand. A gift, freely given. Aslan felt a warmth spread through his whole body. He understood now: kindness always returns to you, brighter and warmer than before. The Phoenix cried out one long, beautiful call that echoed across the mountains, and rose blazing into the sky.
