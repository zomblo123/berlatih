Around My Neighborhood
📖 Tempat-tempat di sekitar rumah & cara memberi arah 🔤 Prepositions of Place 📚 There is / There are
Bacaan

Hi! I am Aslan and I live in Bandung. I love my neighborhood. It is a friendly and busy place. Today I want to tell you about the places around my house.

My house is on a quiet street. Next to my house, there is a small warung — a small food stall. My family often buys snacks and drinks there. The owner is very friendly.

In front of my house, there is a small park. Children play there in the afternoon. I sometimes play there with my friends after I walk home from school. My little sister Alana loves going to the park too, but she needs someone to hold her hand!

Near my house, there is a mosque. Every morning, I can hear the call to prayer. Behind the mosque, there is a market. My mother Fety goes to the market every Saturday morning to buy fresh vegetables and fish.

My school is not far from home. It is between the park and a small clinic. Because it is close, Kakek Cecep takes me there by motorcycle every morning. Going home, I walk with my friends and we pass the warung — we always stop to buy snacks!

I really love my neighborhood. There are many friendly people here. There is always something happening on our street. It feels warm and safe. I am happy to live here in Bandung!

Grammar Fokus
✦ There is / There are + Prepositions of Place
There is a park near my house.
→ There is + satu benda (singular)
There are many shops on my street.
→ There are + banyak benda (plural)
The warung is next to my house.
→ tempat + is + preposition + tempat lain
The school is between A and B.
→ between selalu diikuti "and"
Is there a market near here?
→ bentuk pertanyaan: Is there / Are there + ...?
💡 Ingat: Gunakan There IS untuk satu benda — "There is a mosque." Gunakan There ARE untuk lebih dari satu — "There are many trees." Jangan balik: bukan "Is there a mosque" untuk kalimat biasa, tapi hanya untuk pertanyaan!
Percakapan
Teman — Rafi
Aslan, what is your neighborhood like?

My neighborhood is really nice! It is quiet and friendly. There are many trees and there is a small park in front of my house. I love it here in Bandung!
Teman — Rafi
Is there a market near your house?
Aslan
Yes! There is a market behind the mosque near my house. My mother Fety goes there every Saturday to buy fresh vegetables and fish. The market is always busy and fun!
Teman — Rafi
Where is your school exactly?
Aslan
My school is between the park and a clinic. It is very close to my house! Kakek Cecep takes me there every morning by motorcycle. And I walk home with my friends — we always stop at the warung next to my house for snacks!

Do you like your neighborhood?

Yes, I love it! There are friendly people everywhere. It feels very safe and warm. I am really happy to live here with my family!
