Chapter One
🐚 The Mysterious Seashell
✅ Read! One sunny morning, Aslan was walking alone on the beach. The waves were soft and the sand was warm under his feet. Suddenly, something caught his eye. Half buried in the sand was a huge seashell. It was unlike any shell he had seen before. It glowed with a soft blue light. Aslan picked it up and held it to his ear. He heard not just the ocean, but a gentle voice. "Aslan… follow the blue light. There is a world waiting for you beneath the waves." Aslan looked at the glowing shell and smiled. He was not afraid. He was ready.

Chapter Two
🌊 Diving into the Deep
✅ Read! Aslan stepped into the ocean. The shell in his hand began to pulse with light. As soon as his head went under the water, something magical happened. He could breathe! The shell was protecting him. He swam deeper and deeper. All around him, colourful fish darted past like tiny rockets. Tall green seaweed swayed gently in the current. Thousands of tiny bubbles floated up toward the surface. The water glowed in shades of blue and green. Then, far below, Aslan saw something breathtaking — the towers of an enormous, shimmering castle rising up from the ocean floor.

Chapter Three
🪸 The Coral Castle
✅ Read! The castle was enormous. Its walls were made of white coral that glowed like moonlight. The tall towers were wrapped in pink and orange coral. The windows shone with a warm golden light. Beautiful sea flowers bloomed all around the gates. Aslan swam slowly through the great gate. Inside, the hallways sparkled. Every wall was covered in pearls and colourful gems. Schools of tiny silver fish swam through the corridors like living ribbons. Aslan turned slowly in a circle, looking at everything with wide eyes. "I have never seen anything so wonderful," he whispered.

04
Chapter Four
🐙 The Friendly Sea Creatures
✅ Read! Deep inside the castle, Aslan found a great hall. It was full of sea creatures. A large purple octopus was painting pictures with its eight arms. A friendly green sea turtle was reading a book in the corner. A tiny yellow seahorse waved at Aslan happily. None of them were afraid of him. The octopus smiled with all eight arms open wide. "Welcome, land child! We have been waiting for you. This castle belongs to every creature that is kind and brave. You are one of us now." Aslan felt a warm feeling spread through his chest. He had found new friends in the most unexpected place.

05
Chapter Five
💎 The Treasure of Friendship
✅ Read! The turtle set down his book and led Aslan to a room deep inside the castle. In the centre of the room sat a large treasure chest. Aslan opened it carefully. Inside, there were no coins or jewels. Instead, it was filled with small glowing stones. Each stone had a name carved into it. The turtle explained gently. "Each stone is a friend. The more friends you have — in the ocean, on land, and everywhere — the richer you become." The turtle placed a new glowing stone in Aslan's hand. It was warm and bright. Aslan looked at it and saw his own name carved on it. He carried that stone home in his heart forever.
