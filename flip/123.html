The World of Social Media
📖 Mengungkapkan pendapat, keuntungan, dan risiko secara seimbang 🔤 Opinion & Discussion Essay (B1) 📚 Opinion Phrases + Connectives of Contrast
Bacaan

If you asked most teenagers today what they do first thing in the morning, the answer would probably involve a phone. Social media has become such a significant part of daily life that it is almost impossible to imagine a world without it. But is this a good thing? In my opinion, the answer is both yes and no.

On the one hand, social media has some remarkable benefits. It connects people across enormous distances — I can talk to my pen pal Daniel in Yogyakarta as easily as if he were sitting next to me. It also spreads information at extraordinary speed. During emergencies or important events, news reaches people within minutes. Furthermore, social media has empowered many young people to share their talents — their music, art, writing — with audiences they would never have reached otherwise. Ayah Sigit often says that platforms like YouTube have changed the music industry entirely.

On the other hand, there are serious concerns that cannot be ignored. One of the biggest is the issue of comparison. When we scroll through carefully edited photos and highlight reels of other people's lives, it is easy to feel that our own lives are somehow less exciting or less valuable. Studies suggest that spending too much time on social media can negatively affect young people's self-esteem and mental health. Another concern is misinformation — false news and rumours spread just as quickly as the truth, and it is not always easy to tell the difference.

There is also the question of time. I will be honest: there have been evenings when I intended to watch one video and suddenly it was past ten o'clock. Social media is designed to keep you engaged — and it does its job very well, perhaps too well.

Nevertheless, I do not think the solution is to avoid social media completely. The key, I believe, is to use it consciously. Ask yourself: am I using this platform, or is this platform using me? Set time limits. Choose the content you follow carefully. Be sceptical of information that seems too dramatic or too perfect. And most importantly — remember that real life, with all its imperfections, is always richer than any highlight reel.

Ibu Fety once told me: "A tool is only as good as the person using it." I think that is the perfect way to think about social media. It can be a window to the world — or a mirror that only shows you what you already want to see. The choice, in the end, is yours.

Sisi Terang & Sisi Gelap Social Media
✅ Keuntungan (Pros)
+
Menghubungkan orang lintas jarak — teman, keluarga, pen pal.
+
Menyebarkan informasi dan berita dengan sangat cepat.
+
Memberdayakan anak muda untuk berbagi bakat ke dunia.
+
Membuka peluang belajar — tutorial, kursus, inspirasi.
+
Mengubah industri kreatif — musik, seni, penulisan.
❌ Kerugian (Cons)
−
Perbandingan diri dengan orang lain → merusak rasa percaya diri.
−
Penyebaran misinformasi — berita palsu menyebar secepat fakta.
−
Menyedot waktu tanpa disadari (doomscrolling).
−
Dirancang untuk membuat ketagihan — sulit berhenti.
−
Dampak negatif pada kesehatan mental remaja.

Grammar Fokus
✦ Connectives of Contrast & Addition — Kunci Argumen yang Seimbang
On the one hand ... On the other hand ...
→ Pasangan klasik untuk menyajikan dua sisi argumen. Selalu dipakai bersama.
However / Nevertheless / Yet
→ Kontras yang lebih kuat — dipakai di awal kalimat baru, diikuti koma. "Nevertheless, I disagree."
Furthermore / In addition / Moreover
→ Menambahkan poin baru yang mendukung argumen sebelumnya. "Furthermore, it empowers creators."
Although / Even though + klausa
→ Kontras dalam satu kalimat. "Although it connects people, it can also isolate them."
It can be ... or ... The choice is yours.
→ Penutup opini yang kuat — menyerahkan kesimpulan kepada pembaca. Efektif dan dewasa!
💡 Struktur Opinion Essay B1 yang sempurna:
1️⃣ Intro: Kenalkan topik + nyatakan posisimu (In my opinion...)
2️⃣ Pros: On the one hand... + Furthermore...
3️⃣ Cons: On the other hand... + However...
4️⃣ Conclusion: Nevertheless... + The key is to... + penutup yang berkesan

Ingat: Argumen yang baik mengakui dua sisi — bukan hanya satu. Itulah yang membuat tulisan terasa dewasa dan meyakinkan!
Percakapan
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Aslan, do you use social media a lot? I've been thinking about it lately — I feel like I spend way too much time on it.
Aslan
Honestly? Yes, more than I'd like to admit! There are evenings when I plan to watch one short video and then suddenly it's past ten. I think social media is genuinely designed to pull you in and keep you there.
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Exactly! But it's not all bad, right? I mean, we found each other through an online pen pal programme.
Aslan
That's a great point! On the one hand, it connects people across huge distances — like us. On the other hand, I sometimes feel worse about myself after scrolling for too long. Seeing everyone's "perfect" lives makes my ordinary day feel boring, even when it isn't.
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
I feel that too. What do you do about it?
Aslan
I've started being more conscious about it. I unfollow accounts that make me feel bad about myself, and I try to set a limit — maybe thirty minutes in the evening. Ibu Fety says: a tool is only as good as the person using it. I keep thinking about that.
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
That's really wise! Do you follow any good accounts? What kind of content do you like?
Aslan
I love music production channels — Ayah Sigit introduced me to some amazing ones. Also science explainers and language learning content. Actually, documenting this flipbook journey has made me think about starting my own channel someday. Sharing what I learn, you know? 🎬
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
YES! Do it, Aslan! I would be your first subscriber. Seriously.
Aslan
Haha! Maybe after I finish the 30 days. That would be a perfect first video — "How I learned English in 30 days." And I'd credit Daniel from Yogyakarta as the best pen pal in the world. 😄

Tulis Opinion Paragraph Pertamamu!

— "Should students use phones in the classroom?"
— "Is online learning as good as classroom learning?"
