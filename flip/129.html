Hari 29 / 30 B1 Intermediate ⭐ Hampir Finish! 🌟 Tokoh Inspirasi
A Person I Admire
📖 Mendeskripsikan tokoh yang dikagumi dengan detail dan alasan yang kuat 🔤 Descriptive & Opinion Writing 📚 Relative Clauses + Opinion Phrases
Bacaan

There are many people in this world who are talented, successful, or famous. But the person I admire most is not a celebrity or a world leader. He is my father — Ayah Sigit. He is someone whose dedication, creativity, and quiet strength inspire me every single day.

Ayah Sigit is a musician and a sound engineer. He spends his days in his studio, crafting music with deep focus and love. I have seen him work for hours on a single melody, adjusting every note until it feels exactly right. He never rushes anything. He always says: "Good music takes time. Everything worth doing, takes time."

What I admire most about him is his patience. He is never the kind of person who gets angry quickly. When things go wrong — and in music, many things can go wrong — he simply takes a deep breath and starts again. He does not complain. He does not give up. He just continues. I think that kind of resilience is one of the most admirable qualities a person can have.

Ayah Sigit is also generous in ways that are not always seen. He supports our family with his work, but he also supports us with his presence. Some evenings, after a long day in the studio, he comes out just to sit with us, to listen to our stories, and to laugh together. He never makes us feel like we are a burden. That means more to me than anything.

I also respect how he has always encouraged me to follow my curiosity. When I told him I was doing this 30-day English flipbook, he did not just say "good job." He listened carefully to my reading one evening, and then he said: "Your voice sounds confident, Aslan. Keep going." Those four words stayed with me for weeks.

In the future, I hope to be someone like him — not necessarily a musician, but someone who is dedicated to what they love, patient in the face of difficulty, and always present for the people who matter. Ayah Sigit shows me every day what it means to live with purpose and grace. That, to me, is true greatness.

Grammar Fokus
✦ Relative Clauses & Opinion Phrases — Mendeskripsikan Orang dengan Lebih Kaya
He is someone whose patience inspires me.
→ "whose" = milik dia → digunakan untuk orang (possessive)
He is a person who never gives up.
→ "who" menghubungkan orang dengan sifat atau tindakannya
What I admire most is his patience.
→ "What I admire most" = struktur penekanan yang kuat & formal
He always says: "Good things take time."
→ kutipan langsung membuat esai terasa lebih hidup dan personal
I hope to be someone like him.
→ "I hope to + verb" untuk menyatakan harapan dan tujuan
💡 Struktur esai "A Person I Admire":
1️⃣ Perkenalan: Siapa orangnya? Kenapa kamu pilih dia?
2️⃣ Kualitas 1: Satu sifat utama + contoh nyata dari hidupnya
3️⃣ Kualitas 2: Sifat lain + cerita atau momen konkret
4️⃣ Dampak pada dirimu: Bagaimana dia mempengaruhi hidupmu?
5️⃣ Penutup: Apa yang ingin kamu pelajari dari dia?

Kunci: Jangan hanya menyebutkan sifat — berikan contoh nyata! "He is patient" lemah. "He works for hours on one melody without rushing" kuat! 🔥

Aslan
Definitely his resilience. In music, things go wrong all the time — a track doesn't sound right, equipment breaks, ideas disappear. But he never gets angry or gives up. Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Has he ever said something to you that really stayed with you?
Aslan
Yes! One evening I was reading out loud in English as part of this flipbook, and he came to listen. After I finished, he said very simply: "Your voice sounds confident, Aslan. Keep going." Just four words. But I think about them whenever I feel nervous. He has a way of saying exactly the right thing at the right moment.

You're really lucky to have him. Who's your person, Daniel?

I was about to ask you the same thing! But I think the greatest thing Ayah Sigit has taught me is this: you don't need to be famous to be admirable. You just need to do what you love with your whole heart, and be present for the people around you. That is enough — and that is everything. 🌟

Tips Belajar Hari Ini
✍️
Tulis Tentang Seseorang yang Kamu Kagumi!
Hari ini, tulis 3–5 kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris tentang seseorang yang kamu kagumi — boleh anggota keluarga, guru, teman, atau tokoh publik.

Gunakan struktur ini:
✏️ Who: "The person I admire most is..."
✏️ What quality: "What I admire most about [him/her] is..."
✏️ Why / example: "He/She [concrete example]..."
✏️ Impact: "He/She inspires me to..."

Ingat: Berikan contoh nyata! Bukan hanya "dia baik" — ceritakan kenapa dan bagaimana.
