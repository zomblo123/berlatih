Chapter One
📦 The Dusty Old Box
✅ Read! One rainy afternoon, Aslan was exploring the attic of his grandmother's old house. The attic was full of dusty boxes, old paintings, and forgotten things. Aslan moved aside a heavy curtain and found a small wooden box. It was decorated with tiny gold stars and had a little silver key on its side. Aslan blew away the dust and read the tiny inscription on the lid. "Wind the key and close your eyes. Let the music take you on a journey." Aslan held his breath with excitement. He reached out and turned the little silver key.

Chapter Two
🎵 The First Melody
✅ Read! The moment Aslan turned the key, the box opened by itself. A sweet, gentle melody filled the attic. The notes floated out like tiny glowing birds. Each note was a different colour — pink, gold, violet, blue. The dusty attic shimmered and began to dissolve. Aslan closed his eyes just as the inscription said. He felt himself lifted gently off the floor. The music swirled around him like a warm wind. When Aslan opened his eyes again, he was no longer in the attic. He was standing somewhere breathtaking — and the music box was still playing in his hand.

03
Chapter Three
❄️ Dancing in the Snow Kingdom
✅ Read! Aslan was standing in a glittering snow kingdom. Everything was white and silver. Tall ice trees sparkled in every direction. Soft snowflakes drifted down from a violet sky. As the music box played, the snowflakes began to dance in perfect rhythm. Tiny ice creatures — shaped like foxes and rabbits made of crystal — leaped out from between the trees. They circled Aslan and pulled gently at his sleeves. So Aslan danced. He spun in circles, arms wide open, while the snow swirled around him like the most beautiful dream he had ever had.

04
Chapter Four
🎪 The Carnival of Stars
✅ Read! Aslan wound the key a second time. The snow kingdom melted away and a brand new world appeared. This time, Aslan was standing in a spectacular carnival that floated in the middle of the night sky. Bright lanterns of every colour hung between the stars. Star performers juggled comets and balanced on beams of moonlight. A friendly constellation in the shape of a lion winked at Aslan. "Welcome to the Carnival of Stars!" it roared softly. "Here, every note of music becomes a firework in the sky!" And so it did. Every note from the music box burst into a bloom of coloured light across the sky.

05
Chapter Five
🎶 The Power of Music
✅ Read! When the last note faded, Aslan found himself back in the attic. The music box sat quietly in his hands. Aslan looked at it for a long time. He understood now why his grandmother had kept it so carefully. Music is not just sound. Music is memory. Music is imagination. Music is a door that can open any world you dream of. "The worlds the music takes you to," Aslan realised, "they are not outside you. They are inside you — waiting to be heard." He carried the music box downstairs. That evening, he sat by the window and began to compose his very own melody.
