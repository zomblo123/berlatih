Chapter One
🐾 The Strange Cat on the Roof
✅ Read! One evening, Aslan was walking home along the quiet city street. The sky was dark and full of stars. Suddenly, he heard a strange sound coming from the roof of a building. He stopped and looked up. There, on the edge of a tall roof, sat a black cat. But this was no ordinary cat. It was wearing a small dark hat and a little cape. The cat was holding a tiny magnifying glass in its paw. Aslan rubbed his eyes. He could not believe what he saw. The cat turned its head and looked straight at Aslan. Its eyes were bright purple, like two little gems. Then it spoke in a clear, calm voice: "Good evening. I have been waiting for you, Aslan."

Chapter Two
🗣️ The Talking Cat
✅ Read! Aslan's mouth fell open. "You can talk?" he asked. The black cat jumped down from the roof and landed perfectly on the pavement next to Aslan. Up close, Aslan could see that the cat was also wearing tiny round glasses. "Of course I can talk," the cat said proudly. "My name is Shadow. I am a detective. I solve mysteries in this city every night." Aslan looked at Shadow with wide eyes. "A cat detective?" he said slowly. "Yes," said Shadow. "And tonight I need a helper. Cats are good at sneaking around, but I need someone who can ask people questions. Humans trust other humans, you see." Aslan felt a tingle of excitement in his chest. "Okay," he said with a big smile. "I will help you, Shadow!"

Chapter Three
⌚ The Missing Golden Watch
✅ Read! Shadow led Aslan down the street to a small bakery. An old man named Mr. Hassan was standing outside, looking very sad and worried. "What happened?" Aslan asked. Mr. Hassan sighed. "My golden watch is gone! My grandmother gave it to me fifty years ago. I put it on the bench outside my shop this afternoon and now it has disappeared." Shadow jumped up onto the bench and looked at it carefully through the magnifying glass. "Hmm," said Shadow. "I can see a small mark here... and some crumbs..." Aslan looked at the bench too. There were tiny crumbs of bread near the mark. Shadow's purple eyes sparkled. "Aslan," Shadow whispered quietly, "I think I know what happened. But we need to look for two more clues. Are you ready?" Aslan nodded. "Let's go!"

Chapter Four
🔎 Following the Clues
✅ Read! Shadow and Aslan moved quickly through the dark streets. Shadow ran along the tops of walls while Aslan walked below. "Look for something shiny," said Shadow from above. "And listen for a strange sound." They turned a corner and came to the town fountain. The water sparkled under the street lamps. Aslan stopped. He could hear a soft clinking sound. He looked into the fountain and saw something golden at the bottom. "Shadow! I found it!" he called out softly. Shadow leapt down next to him. "Good eyes, Aslan!" The cat looked around carefully. Near the fountain, there was a crow sitting on a lamp post. It was watching them with shiny black eyes. Shadow smiled a small, clever smile. "I thought so," said Shadow. "Crows love anything that glitters. Our thief has been right here the whole time."

Chapter Five
🌟 The Mystery Solved
✅ Read! Aslan carefully reached into the cool fountain and picked up the golden watch. It was still shining beautifully. They brought it back to Mr. Hassan, who jumped with joy. "My watch! Oh, thank you, thank you!" he cried. He hugged Aslan tightly and gave him a whole bag of fresh bread rolls. He even put out a small bowl of warm milk for Shadow. Shadow lapped the milk up delicately and looked very pleased. Walking home, Aslan looked at the cat detective beside him. "Shadow," he said, "how did you know it was the crow?" Shadow chuckled. "The bread crumbs near the bench — crows visit bakeries. The fountain — crows love to drop shiny things into water. And the golden color of the watch matched the crow's beak exactly." Aslan shook his head in wonder. "You are amazing," he said. Shadow winked. "No, Aslan. We are amazing — together. That is what good partners do. They think together, look together, and help others together." Aslan smiled all the way home.
