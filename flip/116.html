My Childhood Memories
📖 Menceritakan kenangan masa kecil dengan kalimat lampau 🔤 Past Simple 📚 Regular & Irregular Verbs
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to share some of my favourite childhood memories. When I think about the past, I always smile. My childhood in Bandung was full of wonderful moments!

When I was very young — about three or four years old — I loved playing in Kakek Cecep's garden. I helped him water the plants every morning. I didn't know the names of the plants then, but I enjoyed the cool morning air in Bandung. Kakek Cecep always gave me a small watering can to use.

When I was five years old, my father Sigit taught me my first guitar chord. I remember I tried very hard but my fingers hurt a lot! I cried a little, but Ayah Sigit said, "Every great musician started from zero, Aslan." That made me feel better. I kept practising.

One of my happiest memories is when Alana was born. I was in primary school at the time. I went to the hospital with Kakek Cecep. When I saw Alana for the first time, she was so tiny and so beautiful. I immediately decided that I would be the best big brother in the world!

I also remember the first time I met Fathan. We were in the first grade together. On the first day of school, I was very nervous and I sat alone. Fathan came to me and said, "Do you want to be my friend?" From that day, we became best friends.

These memories are very precious to me. The past shaped who I am today. I am grateful for every moment — the happy ones and the difficult ones too.

Grammar Fokus
✦ Past Simple — Menceritakan Kejadian di Masa Lampau
I helped Kakek water plants.
→ Regular: kata kerja + -ed (helped, enjoyed, tried)
My father taught me guitar.
→ Irregular: bentuk berubah (teach → taught)
I was very nervous.
→ be → was (I/he/she/it) / were (you/we/they)
I didn't know the names.
→ Negatif: didn't + kata kerja dasar
Did you enjoy your childhood?
→ Pertanyaan: Did + subjek + kata kerja dasar?
When I was five, I learned guitar.
→ When I was + umur + , + past simple
💡 Past Simple digunakan untuk:
① Kejadian yang sudah selesai di masa lalu: "I met Fathan in grade 1."
② Kebiasaan di masa lalu: "I helped Kakek every morning."
③ Urutan kejadian: "Fathan came, sat down, and said hello."

Kunci: Regular verbs + -ed. Irregular verbs harus dihafal satu per satu!

Hi Aslan! Can you tell me about your childhood? What was it like growing up in Bandung?
Aslan
Hi Daniel! My childhood in Bandung was wonderful! When I was little, I loved playing in Kakek Cecep's garden. Every morning I helped him water the plants. The cool Bandung air made it feel so fresh and peaceful.
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
That sounds so lovely! What is your earliest happy memory?
Aslan
When I was about five, my father Sigit taught me my first guitar chord! My fingers hurt a lot and I actually cried a little. But Ayah Sigit said "Every great musician started from zero." I kept practising after that. Now I can play a few songs!
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
How did you meet your best friend Fathan?
Aslan
On the first day of school, I was so nervous — I just sat alone and didn't speak to anyone. Then Fathan came to me and said, "Do you want to be my friend?" Just like that! We became best friends from that day. I was so lucky he sat next to me!
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Aww! And when did Alana arrive?
Aslan
Alana was born when I was already in primary school. I went to the hospital with Kakek Cecep. When I first saw her — so tiny! — I immediately decided I would be the best big brother ever. She will be three in October this year. Time flies so fast!

Tips Belajar Hari Ini

Tulis 5 Kenangan Masa Kecilmu dalam Bahasa Inggris!
Past Simple adalah tense yang paling sering digunakan untuk bercerita. Latihan terbaik adalah menulis tentang dirimu sendiri!

Gunakan pola: "When I was [umur], I [past verb]..."

Contoh:
📝 "When I was four, I helped Kakek Cecep in the garden."
📝 "When I was five, my father taught me guitar."
