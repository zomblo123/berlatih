Animals Around Us
📖 Mendeskripsikan hewan dengan kata sifat sederhana 🔤 Can + ability 📚 Animal Vocabulary
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to talk about animals! I love animals very much. There are many amazing animals in Indonesia and around the world.

In my house in Bandung, we have a cat. Her name is Cilo. She is small, white, and very fluffy. She can jump very high and she can run fast too. Alana loves playing with Cilo — she always chases her around the house and laughs! Cilo cannot swim, but she does not seem to mind.

In Kakek Cecep's garden, there are often birds that visit. They can fly and sing beautifully in the morning. Kakek Cecep loves listening to them while he gardens. Sometimes we also see butterflies and dragonflies near the flowers.

My favourite animal is the eagle. It is the symbol of Indonesia — the Garuda! Eagles can fly very high and they have very sharp eyes. They are strong and brave. I think the eagle is the most magnificent bird in the world.

Indonesia is also famous for its unique animals. The Komodo dragon is the largest lizard in the world and it lives only in Indonesia! The orangutan is a very intelligent animal. It can use simple tools. There are also beautiful dolphins in the seas around Indonesia.

I think it is very important to protect animals and their habitat. Many animals in Indonesia are endangered. We must take care of nature so that animals can live safely. Kakek Cecep always says, "A garden with birds and butterflies is a healthy garden!"

Grammar Fokus
✦ Can + Ability — Menyatakan Kemampuan Hewan (dan Orang!)
A cat can jump high.
→ subjek + can + kata kerja dasar (kemampuan)
A dolphin cannot fly.
→ subjek + cannot/can't + kata kerja (tidak bisa)
Can an eagle swim?
→ Can + subjek + kata kerja? (pertanyaan)
Yes, it can. / No, it can't.
→ jawaban singkat: Yes/No + it/they + can/can't
Elephants can swim very well.
→ tambahkan adverb: very well / fast / high
💡 Ingat: Setelah can atau cannot selalu gunakan kata kerja dasar — tanpa -s, tanpa -ing, tanpa -ed!
✅ "A cat can jump."  ❌ "A cat can jumps."  ❌ "A cat can jumping."

Can juga dipakai untuk manusia: "I can speak English!" 🎉
Percakapan
Fathan
Hey Aslan! Do you have any pets at home?
Aslan
Yes! We have a cat named Cilo. She is small, white, and very fluffy. She can jump really high and run super fast. My little sister Alana loves chasing her around the house — it's so funny to watch!
Fathan
Haha! Can Cilo swim?
Aslan
No, she can't! Cats generally don't like water at all. Cilo runs away the moment she sees a bucket of water. It's hilarious! But she can climb very well — she sometimes climbs Kakek Cecep's garden fence!
Fathan
What is your favourite animal, Aslan?
Aslan
My favourite animal is the eagle! It can fly very high, it has sharp eyes, and it is the symbol of Indonesia — the Garuda! I think it's the most magnificent bird in the world. What about you?
Fathan
I like dolphins! Can they really jump out of the water?
Aslan
Yes, they can! Dolphins can jump really high out of the water. They are also very intelligent and can communicate with each other. Indonesia has beautiful dolphins in the seas around Lombok and Raja Ampat. I really want to see them one day!

Tips Belajar Hari Ini
🐾
Buat Deskripsi Hewan Favoritmu dalam Bahasa Inggris!
Pilih hewan favoritmu dan tulis deskripsi pendek menggunakan pola:

"A [hewan] is [adjective]. It can [kemampuan 1] and [kemampuan 2]. It cannot [kemampuan yang tidak bisa]. It lives in [habitat]."

Contoh: "An eagle is strong and magnificent. It can fly very high and hunt. It cannot swim. It lives in the forest and mountains."

💡 Tantangan: Coba deskripsikan Cilo si kucing kepada Alana dalam bahasa Inggris! Alana pasti senang mendengarnya walaupun belum mengerti — latihan tetap latihan! 🐱
