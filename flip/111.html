My School Day
📖 Mata pelajaran, jadwal & bahasa di dalam kelas 🔤 What time does school start? 📚 School Subjects
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to tell you about my school day. I go to school five days a week — from Monday to Friday. It is always a busy but exciting day!

Every morning, my grandfather Kakek Cecep takes me to school by motorcycle. We leave home at quarter to seven — that is 06.45. School starts at quarter past seven — 07.15. Because school is close to our house, the ride is short and fun. Kakek Cecep always says, "Study hard, Aslan!" before I go in.

My favourite subject is English! I love learning new words and practising with my friends. I also enjoy Science because I like learning how the world works. My mother Fety always says that Science and English are the most important subjects for the future.

I find Mathematics a little difficult sometimes, but my best friend Fathan is very good at it. He always helps me understand when I get confused. We sit next to each other in class. Sometimes our teacher Miss Dewi asks us to work together, and that is my favourite kind of lesson!

At school, we also have Physical Education — we call it PE. That is when we play sports and exercise outside. I love PE because we sometimes play football! Fathan is the best player in our class. We also have Art class once a week, where we draw and create things.

School finishes at three o'clock in the afternoon. After that, Fathan and I walk home together. We always stop at the warung next to my house to buy snacks. Then I do my homework before dinner. I am always tired by the end of the day — but happy!

Grammar Fokus
✦ What time does school start? — Menanyakan & Menyatakan Waktu Sekolah
What time does school start?
→ What time + does + subjek + kata kerja?
School starts at quarter past seven.
→ Subjek + starts/finishes + at + waktu
School finishes at three o'clock.
→ finish = selesai — pakai -es untuk he/she/it
My favourite subject is English.
→ My favourite subject is + nama pelajaran
I find Maths difficult.
→ I find + pelajaran + adjective (penilaian subjektif)
We have English on Monday.
→ We have + pelajaran + on + hari
💡 Waktu dalam bahasa Inggris:
07.15 = quarter past seven (lewat seperempat tujuh)
06.45 = quarter to seven (kurang seperempat tujuh)
15.00 = three o'clock atau three PM
10.30 = half past ten (setengah sebelas)

Tips Belajar Hari Ini
🏫
Gunakan Bahasa Inggris di Kelas Mulai Besok!
Mulai besok, coba gunakan setidaknya satu ekspresi bahasa Inggris di kelas setiap hari:

🙋 "Can I ask a question?" — saat mau bertanya
🤔 "I don't understand." — saat bingung
🔄 "Can you repeat that, please?" — saat tidak dengar

Juga coba tulis jadwal pelajaranmu dalam bahasa Inggris: "On Monday, I have English and Science. On Friday, we finish early!"

Ceritakan ke Ibu Fety tentang mata pelajaran favoritmu dalam bahasa Inggris malam ini — dia pasti senang mendengarnya sebagai guru! 📚
