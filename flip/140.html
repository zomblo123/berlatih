The Tiny Giant
Friend

A story about Aslan who meets a giant with the biggest body and the kindest heart — and learns that true friendship has nothing to do with size. 🌿👣💚

Chapter One
👣 The Loud Footsteps
✅ Read! One quiet afternoon, Aslan was reading a book under a big tree. Suddenly, the ground began to shake. BOOM! BOOM! BOOM! The footsteps were so loud that Aslan's book jumped right off his lap! The birds flew out of the tree in a panic. Aslan stood up and looked around. The trees were shaking. The ground was trembling. Something very very large was coming closer. Aslan did not run. He was a brave boy. He took a deep breath and waited. Then, from behind the tallest tree, appeared the most enormous creature he had ever seen.

Chapter Two
🌿 Meeting the Tiny Giant
✅ Read! The giant was incredibly tall — as tall as three houses stacked on top of each other. His eyes were as big as dinner plates, and they were the warmest shade of green. He had wild dark hair, a round nose, and rosy cheeks. When he smiled, his teeth were like white boulders. He looked down at Aslan and said, "Oh! A tiny human! Hello, tiny human! My name is Grum." His voice was like thunder, but his smile was the kindest thing Aslan had ever seen. Aslan laughed and waved. My name is Aslan! I am NOT tiny! The giant giggled. It sounded like a cheerful earthquake.

03
Chapter Three
🎮 Playing Together
✅ Read! Grum sat down very carefully so as not to crush anything. Now his head was only a little higher than Aslan's. They decided to play catch. But when Grum threw the ball, it sailed over three hills and a river! Grum covered his mouth with his giant hand. Sorry! I forgot my own strength! Aslan burst out laughing. So instead, Aslan climbed up onto Grum's shoulder. From up there, he could see the whole valley. The river sparkled. The forest looked like a green carpet. Aslan spread his arms wide and shouted, "I am the king of the world!" Grum laughed so hard that Aslan had to hold on tight.

04
Chapter Four
🌧️ The Big Problem
✅ Read! Later that day, Grum suddenly became very quiet. His big green eyes filled with tears. Each teardrop was the size of a bucket. Aslan was worried. What is wrong, Grum? Grum sniffled. "I have no friends. Everyone runs away from me because I am so big. They think I am scary. But I never want to hurt anyone. I just want to play." Aslan climbed up onto Grum's knee and patted his giant hand gently. I am not afraid of you, Grum. I am your friend. Grum blinked. The giant tears stopped. A slow, wide smile spread across his enormous face.
🔑 Key Words — tap to hear
teardrop bucket sniffled scary patted afraid blinked
05
Chapter Five
💚 A Small Heart in a Big Body
✅ Read! The next day, Aslan brought his friends from the village to meet Grum. At first, they were scared and hid behind Aslan. But Grum sat down slowly, and in a gentle voice he said, "Hello, small friends. I promise I will be very, very careful." One by one, the children came closer. Grum used his giant hand as a slide. He blew gently on the dandelions to make a snowstorm of white seeds. Everyone laughed and cheered. That evening, as the sun set, Aslan sat on Grum's shoulder and thought about what he had learned today. A true friend is not measured by his size. A true friend is measured by the size of his heart — and Grum's heart was the biggest of all.

Well done, Aslan! You have a giant heart! 🦁🌿

Keep going — your heart and your English are both growing bigger every day! 💚
