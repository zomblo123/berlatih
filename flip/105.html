My Hobbies and Free Time
📖 Kegiatan waktu luang & cara membicarakannya 🔤 Gerunds · I enjoy / I love doing 📚 Hobby Vocabulary
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to tell you about my hobbies and what I do in my free time. I have many things that I enjoy doing!

My favourite hobby is reading. I enjoy reading books, especially stories about adventure and science. Every night before bed, I read at least ten pages. Reading helps me learn new words in English too!

I also love listening to music. My father Sigit is a musician, so there is always music in our house. I enjoy listening to many kinds of music. Sometimes I try to play the guitar with my father. He is very patient when he teaches me!

In my free time, I also enjoy watching videos online. I like watching educational videos and funny videos. My father Sigit is a content creator, so he sometimes lets me help him make videos. I find it very exciting!

On weekends, I love spending time with my friends. We enjoy cycling around the neighborhood or playing games together at the park in front of my house. My little sister Alana always wants to come too — she loves watching us play!

My grandfather Kakek Cecep enjoys gardening. He grows vegetables and flowers in our yard. Sometimes I help him water the plants. Enin Ii loves cooking — that is definitely her favourite hobby! And my mother Fety enjoys reading too, just like me. We are a family of readers!

I think hobbies are important. They make us happy and help us relax. What do you enjoy doing in your free time?

Grammar Fokus
✦ Gerunds — Kata Kerja + -ing sebagai Objek
I enjoy reading.
→ enjoy + kata kerja-ing (gerund)
I love cycling.
→ love + kata kerja-ing
She likes cooking.
→ like + kata kerja-ing
He doesn't mind gardening.
→ don't mind + kata kerja-ing
I can't stand waiting.
→ can't stand + kata kerja-ing (tidak tahan)
💡 Gerund = kata kerja + -ing yang berfungsi sebagai kata benda.
Setelah kata: enjoy, love, like, hate, mind, miss, finish, stop — selalu gunakan -ing, bukan kata kerja dasar.
✅ "I enjoy reading."  ❌ "I enjoy read."
⚡ Verb + Gerund — Daftar Penting
enjoy + -ingI enjoy listening to music.
love + -ingShe loves cooking for us.
like + -ingHe likes playing guitar.
hate + -ingI hate waking up late.
finish + -ingI finished reading the book.
Percakapan
Teman — Rafi
Aslan, what do you enjoy doing in your free time?
Aslan
I enjoy reading books — especially adventure stories! I also love cycling around the neighborhood with my friends after school. It's so refreshing!
Teman — Rafi
Wow! Do you have any other hobbies?
Aslan
Yes! I love listening to music. My father Sigit is a musician, so there is always music at home. He's teaching me to play guitar. I'm not very good yet, but I enjoy practising!
Teman — Rafi
That's so cool! What about your family — do they have hobbies too?
Aslan
Yes! Kakek Cecep loves gardening — he grows vegetables in our yard. Enin Ii loves cooking, of course! My mother Fety enjoys reading just like me. And my baby sister Alana? She loves watching everyone do everything!
Teman — Rafi
Haha! Is there anything you hate doing?
Aslan
Hmm… I hate waking up late on school days! And I don't enjoy doing the dishes after dinner. But I don't mind helping Kakek Cecep water the plants. That's actually fun!
