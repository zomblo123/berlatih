When I Was Young...
📖 Membandingkan kehidupan dulu & sekarang 🔤 Used to + Past vs Present 📚 Contrast & Comparison
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to compare my life then and now. It is interesting to think about how much things have changed as I have grown up!

When I was very young, I used to wake up very late — sometimes at eight or nine in the morning! But now, I wake up at six o'clock every day because I have school. I used to take a long nap every afternoon too. These days, I rarely have time for that. Life is busier now!

I used to be afraid of the dark. When I was small, Enin Ii used to leave a small light on in my room every night. Nowadays, I am not afraid anymore. I sleep in the dark just fine — although our cat Cilo sometimes jumps on my bed in the middle of the night and gives me a fright!

When I was young, I used to follow Ayah Sigit everywhere. He used to take me to his recording sessions and let me sit quietly in the corner. I didn't understand anything about music then. But now, I understand chords, rhythms, and melodies. I can even play a few songs on the guitar myself!

Ibu Fety used to read me bedtime stories every single night. My favourite was always a story about a brave young explorer. Nowadays, I read by myself — and I choose my own books. I used to only read picture books. But now I read full novels and even books in English!

Some things have stayed the same though. Kakek Cecep still tends his garden every morning, just like always. Enin Ii still cooks the most delicious food. And I still love my family just as much — maybe even more, because now I understand how lucky I am.

Pola "Used to" — 4 Bentuk
✦ Used to — Kebiasaan / Kondisi di Masa Lalu yang Sudah Tidak Terjadi Lagi
✅ Positif
I used to wake up late.
Dulu saya biasa bangun kesiangan. (sekarang sudah tidak)
❌ Negatif
I didn't use to read novels.
Dulu saya tidak biasa membaca novel. (sekarang sudah membaca)
❓ Pertanyaan
Did you use to be afraid of the dark?
Apakah dulu kamu takut gelap?
🔄 Orang lain
Ibu Fety used to read me stories every night.
Ibu Fety biasa membacakan cerita setiap malam. (dulu, tidak lagi)

Grammar Fokus
✦ Used to — Membandingkan Masa Lalu & Sekarang
I used to wake up late.
→ used to + kata kerja dasar (kebiasaan masa lalu)
I didn't use to read novels.
→ didn't use to + kata kerja dasar (negatif)
Did you use to be scared?
→ Did + subjek + use to + kata kerja? (pertanyaan)
I used to …, but now I …
→ pola kontras: dulu ↔ sekarang
Kakek still tends his garden.
→ still = masih terjadi sampai sekarang
I'm not afraid anymore.
→ not … anymore = sudah tidak lagi
💡 Used to vs Past Simple:
Used to = kebiasaan atau kondisi yang berlangsung lama di masa lalu, dan sekarang sudah berubah.
"I used to wake up late." (kebiasaan berulang — sudah berubah)

Past Simple = kejadian spesifik yang terjadi sekali.
"Yesterday I woke up at six." (satu kejadian kemarin)

✅ "I used to be afraid."  ❌ "I used to was afraid." — setelah used to selalu kata kerja DASAR!
Percakapan
Fathan
Aslan, were you very different when you were small? I can't imagine you being a little kid!
Aslan
Haha! Yes, very different! I used to wake up super late — sometimes at nine in the morning! And I used to be really afraid of the dark. Enin Ii always left a light on for me. Can you believe that?
Fathan
Haha! Seriously? Aslan was afraid of the dark? What changed?
Aslan
I just grew up, I guess! Nowadays I'm not afraid anymore. But sometimes Cilo jumps on my bed at night and gives me a fright anyway! What about you Fathan — did you use to be afraid of anything?
Fathan
I used to be terrified of dogs! But now I think they're cool. What else was different about you back then?
Aslan
I used to not understand music at all! I'd go to Ayah Sigit's recording sessions and just sit there confused. But now I understand chords and melody — I can even play guitar a little! And I used to only read picture books. Now I read novels and English books. I've changed a lot!
Fathan
That's amazing growth, Aslan. What has stayed the same though?
Aslan
Kakek Cecep still gardens every morning, Enin Ii still cooks the best food in Bandung, and I still love my family more than anything. Some things never change — and I'm really glad they don't. 😊

Tips Belajar Hari Ini
🔄
Bandingkan Dirimu Dulu dan Sekarang dalam Bahasa Inggris!
Buat daftar 5 hal yang berubah dari dirimu — gunakan pola kontras ini:

"I used to ___, but now I ___."
"I didn't use to ___, but nowadays I ___."

Contoh:
📝 "I used to be afraid of the dark, but now I'm not afraid anymore."
📝 "I didn't use to read English books, but nowadays I read them every day!"

💡 Tanya juga Kakek Cecep atau Enin Ii — "What did you use to do when you were young?" — pasti ceritanya seru dan bisa kamu tulis dalam bahasa Inggris! 🌟
