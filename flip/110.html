Hari 10 / 30 A2 Elementary 💼 Pekerjaan
Jobs and Work
📖 Pekerjaan umum, tempat kerja & tugas sehari-hari 🔤 He/She works as... 📚 Job Vocabulary
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to talk about jobs and work. When I grow up, I want to have an exciting job. But first, let me tell you about the jobs in my family!

My father Sigit has four jobs — he is a businessman, a musician, an IT expert, and a content creator. He works very hard every day. In the morning, he manages his business. In the afternoon, he makes videos and music. He always says, "If you love what you do, it never feels like work!"

My mother Fety is a teacher. She works at SD Cemara, a primary school near our house in Bandung. She teaches young children every day. Being a teacher is a very important job because teachers help shape the future. I am very proud of her!

In my neighborhood, there are many different jobs. The doctor at the clinic near my school helps sick people get better. The seller at the warung next to my house sells food and drinks. The farmer grows vegetables that we buy at the market every Saturday with Ibu Fety.

Kakek Cecep used to work as a civil servant — a government worker. Now he is retired and spends his time gardening. He always says his best job now is being a grandfather! Enin Ii has always been a homemaker — she takes care of the family and cooks the most delicious food.

When I grow up, I want to be a writer or maybe a musician like my father Sigit. Or perhaps a teacher like my mother Fety. The most important thing is to choose a job that makes you happy and helps other people!

Grammar Fokus
✦ He/She works as... — Mendeskripsikan Pekerjaan Seseorang
She is a teacher.
→ He/She + is + a/an + pekerjaan (paling umum)
He works as a musician.
→ He/She + works as + pekerjaan (lebih formal)
She works at SD Cemara.
→ He/She + works at + tempat kerja
He used to work as a civil servant.
→ used to + pekerjaan di masa lalu (sekarang sudah tidak)
What does your father do?
→ Cara bertanya pekerjaan: What does + he/she + do?
I want to be a writer.
→ Menyatakan cita-cita: I want to be + a/an + pekerjaan
💡 Ingat artikel a/an: Gunakan a sebelum konsonan — a teacher, a doctor, a musician. Gunakan an sebelum vokal — an IT expert, an engineer, an artist. Jangan lupa: "What does he/she do?" — bukan "What is his/her work?"
Percakapan
Daniel — Pen Pal dari Yogyakarta
Hi Aslan! In your last letter you mentioned your father has many jobs. What does he do exactly?
Aslan
Hi Daniel! Yes, my father Sigit is very busy! He works as a businessman, a musician, an IT expert, and a content creator. He makes music and YouTube videos too. He always says if you love your work, it never feels like a burden!
Daniel — Pen Pal dari Yogyakarta
Wow, that's amazing! What about your mother? What does she do?
Aslan
My mother Fety works as a teacher at SD Cemara — a primary school near our house. She teaches young children every day. I think being a teacher is one of the most important jobs in the world. I am very proud of her!
Daniel — Pen Pal dari Yogyakarta
What about your grandparents?
Aslan
Kakek Cecep used to work as a civil servant, but now he is retired. He spends his time gardening — he grows our vegetables! And Enin Ii is a homemaker. She takes care of our whole family and cooks the most delicious food. Her job is the hardest of all!
Daniel — Pen Pal dari Yogyakarta
What is your dream job, Aslan?
Aslan
Hmm, I want to be a writer or a musician like my father! Or maybe a teacher like my mother. I haven't decided yet. But I know I want a job that makes me happy and helps other people. What about you, Daniel?

Tips Belajar Hari Ini
💼
Tanya Keluargamu tentang Pekerjaan Mereka dalam Bahasa Inggris!
Malam ini, coba wawancarai anggota keluargamu dalam bahasa Inggris! Gunakan pertanyaan ini:

❓ "What do you do?" (Apa pekerjaanmu?)
❓ "Where do you work?" (Di mana kamu bekerja?)
❓ "Do you like your job?" (Apakah kamu suka pekerjaanmu?)

Lalu tulis jawabannya: "[Nama] works as a [pekerjaan]. He/She works at [tempat kerja]."

Tanya juga Ayah Sigit tentang pekerjaan favoritnya di antara empat pekerjaannya — pasti jawabannya menarik! 🎸
