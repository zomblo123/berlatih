A Trip to the Zoo
📖 Menceritakan kunjungan ke kebun binatang secara runtut 🔤 First, Then, After that, Finally 📚 Past Simple + Sequencing
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Last month, my family went on a very special trip. We visited the Bandung Zoo — Kebun Binatang Bandung. It was an amazing day that I will never forget!

First, we prepared everything in the morning. Enin Ii woke up early and packed delicious food for our picnic. Ayah Sigit checked the car and Ibu Fety helped little Alana get ready. Alana was so excited — she kept saying "zoo! zoo! zoo!" all morning! I also brought a small notebook to write down the animals I saw.

Then, we drove to the zoo. It only took about twenty minutes from our house. When we arrived, Kakek Cecep bought the tickets. The zoo was already busy with many visitors. We walked through the entrance and Alana immediately ran towards the first animal she saw — a big peacock! It spread its beautiful feathers and Alana screamed with delight.

After that, we visited the big animal section. We saw elephants, giraffes, and zebras. Fathan — who I had invited — was most excited about the lions. The lion roared loudly and Fathan jumped back! I laughed so hard. We also stopped at the orangutan area. The orangutans were very funny — one of them grabbed a banana and sat down to eat it, just like a little person.

After that, we found a nice shady spot and had our picnic. Enin Ii's food was, as always, absolutely delicious. Alana fell asleep in Ibu Fety's arms after eating. So cute!

Finally, as the sun began to set, we drove home. Kakek Cecep said it was the best day out he had had in years. I totally agreed. It was a perfect family day!

Grammar Fokus
✦ Past Simple + Sequencing Words — Bercerita secara Runtut
First, Enin Ii packed the food.
→ First + , + past simple (awal kejadian)
Then, we drove to the zoo.
→ Then + , + past simple (kejadian berikutnya)
After that, we saw the lions.
→ After that + , + past simple (setelah itu)
Finally, we drove home.
→ Finally + , + past simple (penutup cerita)
Alana ran and screamed with delight.
→ Dua kejadian berurutan: verb + and + verb (past)
When we arrived, Kakek bought tickets.
→ When + past simple, + past simple
💡 Tip bercerita dalam bahasa Inggris: Gunakan sequencing words untuk membuat ceritamu mengalir dan mudah dipahami. Bayangkan kamu sedang bercerita kepada temanmu — "First... Then... After that... Finally..."

Selalu ingat: di cerita masa lampau, semua kata kerja harus dalam bentuk PAST SIMPLE! Jangan campurkan dengan present tense.
Percakapan
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Hi Aslan! Did you do anything fun recently? Tell me all about it!
Aslan
Hi Daniel! Yes! Last month we went to Bandung Zoo — the whole family plus Fathan! It was the most fun day we've had in a long time. Do you want to hear about it?
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Yes please! Tell me everything — in order!
Aslan
Okay! First, we prepared in the morning — Enin Ii packed amazing food and Alana kept shouting "zoo! zoo!" Then we drove there — only about 20 minutes. Kakek Cecep bought the tickets. After that, we explored all the animal areas. The best part was when the lion roared and Fathan jumped back in shock — I laughed so hard! Finally, we had our picnic and then drove home at sunset. Perfect day!
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
Haha! Poor Fathan! What was Alana's favourite animal?
Aslan
Definitely the peacock! The moment we walked in, she saw this huge peacock spread its beautiful feathers. She screamed with pure delight! Then she fell asleep during the picnic after that — all that excitement wore her out. So adorable!
Daniel — Pen Pal Yogyakarta
That sounds amazing! I want to visit Bandung someday and go to the zoo with you!
Aslan
Yes! You must come! We can visit the zoo, eat Enin Ii's cooking, and explore Bandung together. I will be your tour guide! And I promise Fathan will make you laugh the whole time! 😄

Ceritakan Perjalananmu dalam Bahasa Inggris!
Pilih perjalanan atau kegiatan seru yang pernah kamu lakukan — bisa ke mall, ke rumah nenek, atau ke sekolah pertama kalinya — lalu tulis dalam bahasa Inggris menggunakan sequencing words:

First, ____. Then, ____. After that, ____. Finally, ____.

Minimal 5 kalimat! Gunakan past simple untuk semua kata kerja. Ceritakan juga ke Fathan — bisa jadi Fathan mau balas cerita tentang perjalanan dia juga! 🎒
