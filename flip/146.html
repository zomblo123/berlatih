Chapter One
🌙 The Hidden Night Market
✅ Read! One clear night, Aslan could not sleep. He went to his window and looked out. Down the narrow street, something caught his eye. Warm golden light was spilling out from an alley he had never noticed before. Aslan pulled on his coat and slipped quietly outside. At the end of the alley was an enormous market. Hundreds of colourful lanterns hung between the stalls. The air smelled of spices and something sweet. Every single stall — every one — sold nothing but carpets. Carpets of every colour, every size, and every pattern imaginable.

Chapter Two
🪄 The Magic Carpets
✅ Read! Aslan walked slowly through the market, staring at everything. The carpets were unlike any he had ever seen. One was deep red with golden birds woven into it. Another was midnight blue with tiny silver stars that actually twinkled. An old merchant with a long white beard smiled at Aslan. "Choose any carpet you like, young traveller. They are all enchanted. Sit on one, think of where you want to go, and it will take you there." Aslan's eyes went wide. He looked left. He looked right. There were so many to choose from. How could he ever decide?

03
Chapter Three
🌠 Flying Over the Market
✅ Read! Aslan chose the deep red carpet with the golden birds. He sat down carefully and crossed his legs. He closed his eyes and thought: I want to fly over the market. At once, the carpet gave a gentle shudder and rose smoothly into the air. Aslan gasped with delight. From above, the market looked like a giant jewellery box, glittering with a thousand lantern lights. Other carpets soared alongside him, carrying laughing riders. The cool night wind rushed through Aslan's hair. He opened his arms wide and shouted for joy, "This is the greatest feeling in the whole world!"

04
Chapter Four
💨 The Carpet Chase
✅ Read! Suddenly, a mischievous little carpet — bright green with orange tassels — zipped past Aslan and snatched his hat right off his head! Aslan laughed. So these carpets had their own personality! He leaned forward on his carpet and gave chase. The green carpet darted left and right, looping between the lanterns, spinning in circles. Aslan's carpet was faster. They swooped low over the stalls and soared high above the rooftops. At last, Aslan's carpet pulled up right alongside the cheeky green carpet. The hat sailed off its tassels and landed perfectly back on Aslan's head. The whole market below cheered.

05
Chapter Five
🎁 The Best Souvenir
✅ Read! As the night grew late, Aslan landed his carpet gently and made his way back to the old merchant. He wanted to take a souvenir home. The merchant smiled and held out a very small carpet — no bigger than a book. It was the most beautiful thing Aslan had ever seen. "Take it," said the merchant. "Whenever you feel that life is ordinary and dull, place it on the floor, sit on it, and remember — adventures are always waiting for those who are brave enough to look." Aslan tucked the carpet carefully under his arm. When he stepped out of the alley, he turned back — but the market was gone. Only the little carpet in his hands proved it had ever been real.
