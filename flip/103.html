What Do You Like to Eat?
📖 Makanan favorit & cara mengungkapkan selera 🔤 I like / I don't like 📚 Food Vocabulary
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I want to talk about food. I really love eating! My family in Bandung eats many kinds of delicious food every day.

For breakfast, I usually eat warm rice with egg or fried tempe. My favourite breakfast is fried rice. Enin Ii makes the best fried rice in the world! Sometimes my mother Fety makes toast with butter and jam. I like toast very much too.

I really like Indonesian food. My favourite food is chicken soup. It is warm and delicious. I also like meatballs — we call it bakso! On weekends, my family often eats bakso together near our house. It is so much fun!

I also like fruit. My favourite fruit is mango. It is sweet and juicy. I also like banana and watermelon. I don't like durian very much because the smell is very strong. But Kakek Cecep loves durian!

For drinks, I like cold milk and sweet tea. I don't like coffee. It is too bitter for me. My father Sigit drinks coffee every morning. He says it helps him work!

Food is important for our body. I always try to eat vegetables and fruit every day, even if I don't always like them. What about you? What is your favourite food?

Grammar Fokus
✦ Mengungkapkan Suka & Tidak Suka — I like / I don't like
I like fried rice.
→ I / You / We / They + like + makanan
I don't like durian.
→ I / You / We / They + don't like + makanan
He likes coffee.
→ He / She / It + likes + makanan
She doesn't like spicy food.
→ He / She / It + doesn't like + makanan
My favourite food is bakso.
→ My favourite food / drink + is + nama makanan
💡 Ingat: Gunakan like (tanpa -s) untuk I / you / we / they. Gunakan likes (dengan -s) untuk he / she / it. Untuk kalimat negatif: don't like atau doesn't like. Jangan tulis "I no like" — itu salah!

Buat Daftar Makanan Favoritmu dalam Bahasa Inggris!
Ambil kertas dan bagi jadi dua kolom: I like... dan I don't like...

Tulis minimal 5 makanan / minuman di setiap kolom. Lalu buat 3 kalimat lengkap seperti:

"My favourite food is ___. I also like ___ and ___. But I don't like ___ because it is too ___."
