Yuk siapkan ucapan ulang tahun dalam bahasa Inggris untuk Ayah!
Bacaan

Hello! My name is Aslan. In my family, we love celebrating special days together. There are so many important days in a year — birthdays, Eid Al-Fitr, Independence Day, and many more. Each one is a chance to be happy, say kind words, and make memories.

The most exciting special day in our family is always a birthday. My father Sigit was born on 27 May 1980. That means this month — in just a few days — is his birthday! He will turn 45 years old. Every year, my mother Fety bakes a chocolate cake and we all sing Happy Birthday together. Last year I wrote him a birthday card in English — Ayah Sigit loved it so much he kept it on his desk!

My mother Fety's birthday is on 8 February 1993. She turned 32 years old this year. On her birthday, Ayah Sigit always surprises her with flowers and I give her a big hug and say "Happy Birthday, Bunda! I love you!" She always cries a little — happy tears, she says!

Our newest family member, my little sister Alana, was born on 4 October 2024. She is only a baby — she does not understand birthdays yet! But we will always celebrate her special day every year. On her first birthday this October, we plan to have a small family party. I am already thinking about what to give her as a present!

Besides birthdays, we also celebrate Eid Al-Fitr with all our family. We wear new clothes, visit grandparents, and say "Eid Mubarak!". On Independence Day, the 17th of August, our neighbourhood has fun competitions. Knowing how to give congratulations and greetings in English makes every celebration even more special.

Dialog 1 — Pesta ulang tahun Ayah Sigit di rumah
27 Mei · Ruang keluarga · Malam hari setelah makan malam
Aslan
Happy Birthday, Ayah Sigit! I hope you have a wonderful day. You are the best father in the whole world!
Ayah Sigit
Thank you so much, Aslan! You made my day. Is that... a birthday card you wrote in English?
Aslan
Yes, Ayah! I wrote it all by myself. "Happy 45th Birthday! Thank you for everything." I also drew a cake and a football for you!
Bunda Fety
Everyone, let's sing! "Happy Birthday to you, Happy Birthday to you, Happy Birthday dear Sigit, Happy Birthday to you!" 🎵
— semua meniup lilin bersama —
Ayah Sigit
Thank you, everyone! This is the best birthday ever. I am so lucky to have this family. Now, let's eat the cake!
Aslan
Congratulations on turning 45, Ayah! You don't look 45 at all — you look like a cool content creator! 😄
Ayah Sigit
Ha ha! That is very kind of you, Aslan. Now I know why you are my favourite son!
🎈
Dialog 2 — Fathan mengucapkan selamat kepada Aslan
Di sekolah · Pagi hari setelah pesta ulang tahun Ayah Sigit
Fathan
Hey Aslan! I heard it was your father's birthday yesterday. Happy Birthday to him! Did you have a party?
Aslan
Yes! We had a small family party at home. Bunda Fety baked a chocolate cake and we all sang Happy Birthday. I gave Ayah a birthday card I wrote in English — he loved it!
Fathan
That's so nice! What did you write in the card?
Aslan
I wrote: "Happy 45th Birthday, Ayah! Thank you for always working hard for our family. You are the best father in the world. With love, Aslan." — Ayah said it was the best gift he received!
Fathan
Wow, that's amazing! You really can write well in English now. Can you teach me how to write a birthday card too?
Aslan
Of course! Start with "Dear [name]," then write "Happy Birthday!" and one nice sentence about the person. Then end with "With love" or "Your friend." Easy, right?

Grammar Fokus
✦ Cara menyebut umur, tanggal lahir, dan ucapan selamat
Happy 45th Birthday!
→ Happy + angka urut + Birthday! (1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th...)
He was born on 27 May.
→ born on + tanggal + bulan → untuk menyebut tanggal lahir
He will turn 45 years old.
→ will turn + umur + years old → untuk usia yang akan datang
I wish you all the best!
→ I wish you + hal baik → pola doa atau harapan untuk seseorang
Thank you for everything.
→ Thank you for + kata benda/gerund → ucapan terima kasih spesifik
You are the best father.
→ You are the + superlative → pujian tertinggi untuk seseorang
💡 Angka urut (Ordinal Numbers) untuk ulang tahun:

1st (first)  ·  2nd (second)  ·  3rd (third)  ·  4th (fourth)  ·  5th (fifth)
21st  ·  22nd  ·  23rd  ·  44th  ·  45th (Ayah Sigit!)  ·  32nd (Bunda Fety!)

🔑 Pola kartu ucapan (Birthday Card):
Dear [nama], → Happy [angka]th Birthday! → [satu kalimat pujian] → With love / Your friend, [nama]
Percakapan
Guru — Miss Dewi
Aslan, is there a special day coming up in your family?
Aslan
Yes, Miss! Ayah Sigit's birthday is on 27 May — that is very soon! He will turn 45 years old. I am already writing a birthday card for him in English. I also want to say "Happy 45th Birthday, Ayah!" in front of the whole family!
Guru — Miss Dewi
That's lovely! Who else in your family has a birthday?
Aslan
Bunda Fety's birthday is on 8 February — she turned 32 this year. And my baby sister Alana was born on 4 October 2024, so her first birthday is coming in October! She doesn't understand English yet, but I will say "Happy 1st Birthday, Alana!" and she will smile because babies love happy voices!
Guru — Miss Dewi
Wonderful! How do you say happy birthday with the person's age in English?
Aslan
"Happy [number]th Birthday!" — like "Happy 45th Birthday" for Ayah, or "Happy 32nd Birthday" for Bunda, or "Happy 1st Birthday" for Alana! The number always comes before "Birthday" and we use ordinal numbers — 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th... 45th! I practised this at home and even Ayah Sigit was impressed!
Guru — Miss Dewi
Perfect! What do you say besides "Happy Birthday"?
Aslan
"I wish you all the best!" and "Have a wonderful day!" — and for Ayah I always say "You are the best father in the world!" because it's true! For Bunda, I say "I love you to the moon and back!" — she cries happy tears every time I say that. It's my favourite phrase! 😊
