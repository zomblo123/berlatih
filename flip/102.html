A Day in My Life
📖 Rutinitas harian dari pagi hingga malam 🔤 Present Simple 📚 Daily Routines · Time Expressions
Bacaan

Hello again! My name is Aslan. Today I want to tell you about my daily routine. Every day is busy but I enjoy it!

I wake up at six o'clock in the morning. First, I take a shower and get dressed. Then, I have breakfast with my family. My mother Fety always prepares warm rice and a delicious side dish. Enin Ii sometimes makes fried rice. It is so yummy!

I go to school at half past six. I usually walk or ride a motorcycle with my grandfather (Kakek Cecep). School starts at seven o'clock. I study many subjects with my friends. I really like learning new things.

School finishes at three o'clock in the afternoon. I walk home with my friends. It is my favourite part of the day! We laugh and talk together on the way home. When I arrive home, I have a late lunch or a snack.

In the afternoon, I do my homework. My father Sigit sometimes helps me when he is not busy with his work. At night, I read a book or watch a video. I go to bed at nine o'clock.

That is my day! It is a busy day, but I am always happy.

Grammar Fokus
✦ Present Simple — Rutinitas & Ekspresi Waktu
I wake up at 5.30.
→ I / You / We / They + kata kerja dasar
School starts at 7 o'clock.
→ He / She / It + kata kerja + -s
I usually walk to school.
→ usually / always / sometimes + kata kerja
In the morning, I have breakfast.
→ in the morning / afternoon / evening / at night
I go to bed at nine o'clock.
→ at + waktu (at 7, at half past six)
💡 Time expressions penting: in the morning (pagi) · in the afternoon (sore) · in the evening (malam awal) · at night (malam) · at + jam (misalnya: at seven o'clock) · half past six = 06.30 · quarter past = lewat 15 menit.

Tips Belajar Hari Ini
⏰
Buat Jadwal Harianmu dalam Bahasa Inggris!
Ambil kertas kosong dan tulis jadwal harianmu sendiri menggunakan pola:

"I wake up at ___ . Then I ___ . At ___ , I ___ ."

Gunakan kata-kata seperti first, then, after that, finally untuk menghubungkan kegiatanmu. Tempel di kamar dan baca setiap pagi — dalam satu minggu kamu akan hafal tanpa sadar! 🚀 Keep going, Aslan!
