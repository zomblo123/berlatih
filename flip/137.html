Chapter One
🌙 The Midnight Whistle
✅ Read! Aslan could not sleep. The moon was big and bright outside his window. He lay in bed, watching the stars one by one. Then, at exactly midnight, he heard a sound. It was a long, sweet whistle — like music from far away. Aslan jumped out of bed and ran to his window. Down in the empty field behind his house, two shining silver rails appeared on the ground. They glowed with soft moonlight. And coming slowly down the track was the most beautiful train he had ever seen. Aslan did not wait. He grabbed his coat and ran outside.

Chapter Two
🚂 The Silver Train
✅ Read! The train stopped right in front of Aslan. It was made of deep purple metal that sparkled like the night sky. Its windows glowed with a warm golden light. A door slid open with a soft hiss. Standing inside was a tall conductor in a dark blue uniform covered in tiny star badges. He smiled and said, "Welcome, Aslan. We have been waiting for you." Aslan stepped aboard. The seats were covered in soft velvet. The ceiling was made of clear glass, so he could see all the stars above. Then, with another long whistle, the Moonlight Train began to move.

Chapter Three
⭐ Journey Through the Stars
✅ Read! The train moved faster and faster. Aslan pressed his face against the window. The ground disappeared. The train was rising up into the sky! The moonlight rails stretched up through the clouds and into space. Stars floated all around the train like fireflies. Aslan gasped. He could see the Earth below — blue and green and so beautiful. A shooting star flew past the window, leaving a golden trail. The conductor walked over and said, "Every star has a name, Aslan. Would you like to learn them?" Aslan nodded, unable to speak. He had never felt so small, and yet so alive.

04
Chapter Four
🏙️ The Floating City
✅ Read! The train slowed down and stopped at a station high above the clouds. Outside was a city that floated on giant white clouds. The buildings were made of crystal and reflected the moonlight in every color. Aslan stepped off the train carefully. The cloud ground felt soft and springy under his feet. Children from all over the world were playing there, laughing and chasing each other. A girl with bright eyes ran up to him. "Come and play with us!" she called. "In this city, everyone is a friend." Aslan smiled and ran after her, his heart full of joy.

Chapter Five
🌅 Back Home Before Dawn
✅ Read! The conductor rang a small silver bell. "Last stop, Aslan. The sun will rise soon." Aslan said goodbye to his new friends and climbed back onto the train. The Moonlight Train glided smoothly back down through the stars and clouds. It stopped at the field behind his house. The sky was turning pink and orange. As Aslan stepped off, the conductor handed him a small silver ticket. On it were written three words: "Dream. Be brave. Return." Aslan ran upstairs and jumped into bed. He placed the silver ticket under his pillow. He understood now: the greatest journeys begin when you are brave enough to step outside and say yes. He closed his eyes and smiled. Tomorrow night, the train would come again.
