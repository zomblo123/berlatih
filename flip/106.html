At the Market
📖 Dialog belanja & menanyakan harga barang 🔤 How much / How many 📚 Numbers & Prices
Bacaan

Hello! I am Aslan. Today I went to the market with my mother Fety. She goes to the market every Saturday morning. The market near our house in Bandung is always busy and colourful!

There are many stalls at the market. You can find vegetables, fruit, fish, meat, and many other things there. The sellers are always friendly and the prices are cheap.

My mother Fety bought some vegetables. She asked the seller, "How much is one kilogram of spinach?" The seller said, "It is ten thousand rupiah per kilogram." My mother also asked, "How many tomatoes are in this bag?" The seller said, "About twelve tomatoes."

I helped my mother carry the basket. We bought spinach, tomatoes, carrots, tofu, and some fresh fish. Enin Ii loves cooking with fresh ingredients from the market. She says the food always tastes better when the ingredients are fresh!

I also saw a fruit stall. There were many kinds of fruit — mango, banana, papaya, and watermelon. I asked my mother, "Can I buy some mangoes?" She smiled and said yes. We bought half a kilogram of mangoes for eight thousand rupiah. So cheap and so delicious!

Going to the market with my mother is always fun. I learn about prices, numbers, and how to talk to sellers in English. It is a great way to practise!

Dialog Belanja di Pasar

Ibu Fety & Aslan di Pasar
Sabtu pagi · Pasar dekat rumah di Bandung
Ibu Fety
Excuse me. How much is one kilogram of spinach?
Penjual
It is ten thousand rupiah per kilogram, Bu.
Ibu Fety
How many tomatoes are in this bag?
Penjual
About twelve tomatoes, Bu. Very fresh today!
Ibu Fety
Okay, I'll take one kilogram of spinach and one bag of tomatoes. How much is that in total?
Penjual
That is eighteen thousand rupiah, Bu. Thank you!
Aslan
Mum, can we also buy some mangoes? How much are the mangoes?
Penjual
Eight thousand rupiah for half a kilogram, Dek. Very sweet!
Aslan
Yes please! Here is the money. Thank you!

Grammar Fokus
✦ How much / How many — Menanyakan Harga & Jumlah
How much is the spinach?
→ How much + is/are + benda tak terhitung
How many tomatoes are there?
→ How many + benda terhitung (bisa dihitung)
It costs ten thousand rupiah.
→ It costs + harga (menyatakan harga)
How much does it cost?
→ Pertanyaan harga formal: How much does it cost?
That is Rp 18,000 in total.
→ in total = jumlah keseluruhan
🔵 How MUCH
Untuk benda yang tidak bisa dihitung
water, rice, money, spinach, milk
"How much water?"
"How much is this?"
🟡 How MANY
Untuk benda yang bisa dihitung
tomatoes, mangoes, eggs, bags
"How many tomatoes?"
"How many bags do you need?"
💡 Kunci: Gunakan How much untuk menanyakan HARGA (selalu!) dan untuk benda yang tidak bisa dihitung. Gunakan How many untuk benda yang bisa dihitung dan punya bentuk jamak (-s).

Hey Aslan, where did you go this morning?
Aslan
I went to the market with my mother Fety! We go every Saturday morning. The market near our house is always busy and fun. There are so many stalls!
Teman — Rafi
What did you buy? How much did you spend?
Aslan
We bought spinach, tomatoes, carrots, tofu, and fresh fish for Enin Ii to cook. And I bought some mangoes — half a kilogram for only eight thousand rupiah! So cheap!
Teman — Rafi
How do you ask for the price in English?
Aslan
Easy! You say "How much is this?" or "How much does it cost?" And if you want to know the quantity, you say "How many [items] are there?" Try it next time you go shopping!
Teman — Rafi
That's so useful! I want to try shopping at the market in English too!
Aslan
Come with us next Saturday! The sellers are very friendly. Even if your English is not perfect, they will still understand you. Just be brave and try! 😄
